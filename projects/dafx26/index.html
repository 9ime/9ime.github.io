SEND: A Spatial Event Neural Detector for Intentional Object Motion in Immersive Music Mixing

Anonymous Authors

Anonymous Institution

We present SEND, a dual-stream neural architecture that automates professional-grade spatial metadata generation for object-based immersive music formats. Unlike reactive signal-unmasking approaches, SEND captures macroscopic musical intentionality, allowing audio objects to transition seamlessly in alignment with structural boundaries and emotional arcs of the piece.
