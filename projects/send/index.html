SEND: A Spatial Event Neural Detector for Intentional Object Motion in Immersive Music Mixing

Xu Gan ,Linhao Zhao,Zhenhai Yan

Tencent Music Entertainment

We present SEND,(Spatial Event Neural Detector), a dual-stream architecture that mimics an engineer’s holistic listening by decoupling audio into a target stream (the dy- namic object) and a background context stream (the static accom- paniment mix). .
